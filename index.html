Systems & Software

    (hide)
  • Google Belay is exploring secure sharing and identity management in the cloud. The code for our demo is open source and available. With Arjun Guha, Mark Lentczner, and Úlfar Erlingsson.
    (hide)
  • I designed a novel type-checking strategy for the verification of reference monitors. I used it to mechanically verify ADsafe, a web sandboxing framework, with Arjun Guha, Spiridon Eliopoulos, and Shriram Krishnamurthi.
  • Assistment is tutoring and assessment software for middle school math students, and part of learning sciences research at WPI CS with Neil Heffernan and many others. A pile of JavaScript I wrote has been running parts of Assistment's interactive tutor since 2009.
